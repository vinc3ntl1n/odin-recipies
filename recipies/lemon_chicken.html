Simple Lemon Herb Chicken is a quick, flavorful dish made with chicken breasts, fresh lemon juice, olive oil, and herbs like oregano and parsley. Itâ€™s light, zesty, and perfect for an easy, healthy meal.

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • lemon
  • salt/pepper
  • olive oil
  • oregano
  • parsley

Steps

  1. mix the chicken with the lemon and salt
  2. With a pan on medium heat, place the chicken in hot oil and add the lemon juice, oregano, and black pepper
  3. Garish the chicken with parsley
