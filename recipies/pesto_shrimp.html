Pesto Shrimp

Home

This dish features linguine tossed with shrimp cooked in a rich, creamy sauce flavored with butter, heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, and pesto—ready in about 30 minutes and serving 8 people

Ingredients

  • linguine pasta
  • butter
  • heavy cream
  • black pepper
  • parmesan cheese
  • pesto
  • large shrimp

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta until tender. At the same time boil the shrimp
  2. melt the butter in a skillet over medium. Stir in the cream and pepper as it cooks
  3. Stir the parmesan cheese into the sauce
  4. add the pasta and shrimp and serve
